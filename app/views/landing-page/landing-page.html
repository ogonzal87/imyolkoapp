Quantify feelings

Giving and receiving feedback happens less than it should and often comes too late. Yolko an interactive graphical representation of any presentation’s mood in real time. He shows the collective emotion.

Disrupt a presentation

The delivery of a message is what counts when presenting an idea or subject to an audience. Yolko makes this interaction fun and engaging. The message sticks better when you make it memorable.

Immediate results

All data is 100% real time. This lets the presenter adjust his or her delivery with the audience’s needs, improving their performance. No more blank stares when the presenter ask: “Any questions...?”

It’s all about synchronization. Any inputs from the audience are logged and displayed in the Dashboard for the presenter to see in real time.

Take control of questions. The audience can ask questions anonymously. Since they all see the same questions, they can be voted-up collectively.

Strike back. The presenter can also ask questions to the audience in the form of a Pop Quiz. All their answers are then logged to gauge the collective understanding of a subject.
